About

Have you ever thought the president of your club or board member isn't the right person for the job? Well at the WHS Rocketry Club we make sure only the best make it to the top by setting up a system that allows the removal of the president

What makes us different

The main focus of the WHS Rocketry club is to fight a losing battle againest the biggest constant on earth: gravity. We focus on shooting real model rockets into the sky and are part of a small amount of schools around the country competing in the American Rocketry Challenge for a chance to show NASA future rocketeers
